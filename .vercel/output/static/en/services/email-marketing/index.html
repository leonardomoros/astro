Capabilities

Everything you need to grow with email

From acquisition to repurchase: we cover every stage of the cycle with automations that work while you sleep.

Full-cycle automations

Welcome flows, abandoned cart, win-back sequences, and upsell. Every subscriber receives the right message based on their behavior.

Smart segmentation

We segment your list by behavior, funnel stage, geography, and interests. Less volume, more relevance, better conversion.

Persuasive copy & design

Subject lines that get opened, bodies that get read, CTAs that get clicked. Every piece is designed to move subscribers to the next step.

Systematic A/B testing

We test subject lines, send times, calls to action, and designs. Data decides what works — not opinions.

CRM integration

We connect your email platform with HubSpot, Salesforce, ActiveCampaign, or other tools to keep data in sync.

Real-time reporting & analytics

Clear dashboards with open rates, clicks, conversions, and ROI per campaign. You know exactly what every dollar generates.

Process

From strategy to first live flow in 48 hours

A structured process that guarantees relevance, deliverability, and measurable results from the very first send.

1

Audit & strategy

We analyze your current list, historical open rates, competitors, and opportunities. We define goals, KPIs, and the automation roadmap.

2

Segmentation & list hygiene

We clean bounces, segment by behavior, and configure entry triggers for each flow. A healthy list equals better deliverability.

3

Copy, design & setup

We write the copy, design responsive templates, and configure automations in your platform. Everything ready to activate.

4

Launch, measure & optimize

We activate the flows, monitor early results, and adjust subject lines, send times, and segments based on real data.
